About Tasty Aromas

The Company “Tasty Aromas” is a brain child of experienced horticulturist, farmers and traders, who bring in quality fresh vegetables and organic spices, directly from the organic farms of Kerala to the trade platform of www.tastyaromas.com. At “tastyaromas.com”, the conglomeration of agri-business stakeholders like Farmers, Horticulturist & Traders make available and affordable the freshest of the fresh vegetables and spices any season anywhere.

Vision:

Grow, Trade & Flourish Organic Spices & Fresh Vegetables…….. Anywhere Anytime Any Season.

Mission:

To Toe a Trendsetting Agricultural Practice that cobwebs a network platform called “tastyaromas.com” for all Stake Holders that includes all producers, traders & consumers.
